Vietnam Destinations – More Info

🕌 Hanoi

Vietnam’s ancient capital blends tradition and modern life. Explore the Old Quarter’s vibrant food scene, stroll around Hoan Kiem Lake, and visit historic temples. Don’t miss the famous Train Street!

Learn more about Hanoi

🏞️ Ha Long Bay

A UNESCO World Heritage site, Ha Long Bay is famed for its towering limestone islands and emerald waters. Enjoy boat cruises, kayaking, and cave adventures.

Learn more about Ha Long Bay

🏖️ Nha Trang

Famous for its beaches, islands, and nightlife. Top spot for snorkeling, diving, and fresh seafood. Great for both backpackers and luxury travelers.

Learn more about Nha Trang

🏜️ Mui Ne

A laid-back beach town with famous sand dunes, kite surfing, and jeep tours. See the local fishing villages and try fresh seafood.

Learn more about Mui Ne

🏙️ Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)

Vietnam’s bustling metropolis with lively markets, rooftop bars, and rich history. Explore Cu Chi Tunnels and enjoy world-class street food.

Learn more about Ho Chi Minh City

🌴 Phu Quoc Island

Pristine beaches, crystal waters, and tropical jungles. Great for snorkeling, diving, and sunset BBQs.

Learn more about Phu Quoc Island

🏮 Hoi An

Lantern-lit ancient town. Try custom tailoring, riverside cafes, cooking classes, and basket boat tours.

Learn more about Hoi An

🏔️ Da Nang & Ba Na Hills

A modern beach city with mountain views and the famous Golden Bridge. Family-friendly theme parks and stunning nature.

Learn more about Da Nang

🌾 Mekong Delta

Experience Vietnam’s river life, floating markets, and local homestays among lush scenery.

Learn more about Mekong Delta
